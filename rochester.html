    in Rochester, New York

    PITTSFORD DAIRY

    No summer day is complete without a trip to Pittsford Dairy. The dairy's homemade ice cream steals the show, boasting an array of flavors that cater to every palate. Its welcoming atmosphere and friendly service also make it a great spot to go with friends and family. I love their cookies 'n cream ice cream, but also be sure to try their chocolate milk. You might even see a Buffalo Bills player or two in August during their training camp!

    JINES RESTAURANT

    Jines is a local favorite in downtown Rochester. They offer a wide variety of comforting classics and diverse breakfast and lunch options. What sets Jines apart is not just the food but the overall experience. The bustling vibe might mean a wait during peak hours, but it's a testament to the restaurant's popularity. The staff remains attentive and friendly even when busy, ensuring a pleasant dining experience.

    SIMPLY CREPES

    Simply Crepes is a great place to grab a meal with friends. Located right in village of Pittsford, you can also go on a nice walk along the Erie Canal afterwards. Whether you prefer the sweetness of Nutella and fresh fruit or the savory delights of ham and cheese, each crepe is expertly crafted and bursting with deliciousness. Be sure to check this place out when you're in town!

    DINOSAUR BBQ

    You might not expect Rochester, NY to have a good barbeque restaurant of all places but without a doubt this is one of the best I've had. From the moment you step inside, the aroma of smoked meats fills the air, setting the stage for a mouthwatering meal. The menu at Dinosaur BBQ features a tantalizing array of smoked meats, from tender ribs to juicy brisket. The sauces, ranging from tangy to spicy, add an extra punch to the already flavorful dishes. And don't overlook the sides, which I might argue I prefer over the main dishes â€” classic favorites like mac and cheese, coleslaw, and cornbread perfectly complement the main courses.

    TAICHI BUBBLE TEA

    This local boba shop is a great place to hangout with friends and grab a drink and sushi burrito, poke bowl, or ramen. Their ingredients are always fresh and the vibes are good. They also have a wide assortment of board games to play with your friends and fun playlist going in the background. I like their taro milk tea paired with a poke bowl and would reccomend it to everyone.

    PI CRAFT PIZZA

    Pi Craft Pizza offers a fresh and innovative take on the classic pizza experience. What sets Pi Craft apart is its build-your-own concept, allowing diners to create their personalized pizzas from scratch. What I like about this place is that the pizza is cooked quickly and you know that everything on it will be of liking to you. The restaurant's modern and sleek ambiance provides a casual and welcoming atmosphere. It's an ideal spot for families, groups of friends, or individuals seeking a quick and delicious meal.

    FATTOUSH RESTAURANT

    Fattoush Restaurant opened in early 2023 but it's definitely become one of the fastest growing restaurants in Rochester. Everything on the menu from the chicken shwarma to the falafel is authentic and delicious. The ingredients are fresh, everything is flavorful, and you certainly won't be dissapointed. You'll feel great supporting a small business as well as enjoy a delicious meal.

    VILLAGE BAKERY & CAFE

    Whenever I go home, my friends and I always grab brunch at Village Bakery & Cafe. It's a charming spot that exudes warmth from the moment you walk in. The aroma of freshly baked goods greets you, setting the stage for a delightful culinary experience. The bakery has everything from flaky croissants to decadent cakes and pies and the cafe serves a variety of delicious sandwiches and classic breakfast sweets like french toast. The staff at Village Bakery and Cafe contribute to the welcoming atmosphere, offering friendly service that adds to the overall charm of the place.

    WEGMANS

    Wegmans is not only the best grocery store but they also serve some of the best prepared food. The first thing you'll notice is the sheer variety of options. From international cuisines to comfort food classics, Wegmans' offerings are crafted to satisfy a wide range of appetites. Whether you're in the mood for sushi, artisanal sandwiches, gourmet salads, or hot entrees, there's something for everyone. The convenience factor is another big draw. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch on the go or planning a family dinner, Wegmans' prepared food section caters to busy schedules without compromising on taste or quality.
