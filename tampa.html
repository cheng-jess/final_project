Jessica's Favorite Food Spots

in Tampa, Florida

JENI'S ICE CREAM

Although Jeni's is a nationwide chain, I didn't discover it until my family moved to Tampa. What makes them stand out is their wide selection of unique flavors including my favorites Brambleberry Crisp and Powdered Jelly Donut. One of the locations in Tampa is in Sparkman Wharf so you'll often find yourself enjoying Jeni's in the middle of live music, a movie nights, or watch parties. The quality of Jeni's ice cream is unparalleled. It's rich, creamy, and perfectly balanced in sweetness, making each flavor profile distinct and memorable. Plus, they cater to various dietary needs with options like dairy-free and vegan selections that maintain the same level of flavor and creaminess.

LORTIA'S FRESH MEXICAN FOOD

The moment I land in Tampa, my first stop is always grabbing food at Lorita's, a hidden gem for those seeking authentic Mexican cuisine in the area. The standout feature of Lorita's lies in the freshness of its ingredients and friendlyness of the staff. The flavors are bold and the ingredients are vibrant ensuring a satisfying meal every time. The homemade salsas and sauces add an extra layer of depth to the already delicious dishes. My favorite item is the Carne Asada Chimichanga and not to mention, the portion sizes are generous!

CRABBY'S BAR AND GRILL

Although this restuarant isn't technically in Tampa, it's only a short drive away in Clearwater Beach and the views are beautiful, especially at sunset. I had some of the best fries of my life here and the outside seating allows you to see all the cute dogs that walk by. The menu at Crabby's boasts a variety of seafood options, from fresh catches to classic favorites like crab legs and shrimp.

DIO MODERN MEDITERRANEAN

DIO Modern Mediterranean is a refreshing addition to the culinary scene, offering a contemporary take on traditional Mediterranean flavors. The menu showcases a fusion of vibrant ingredients and bold spices, creating a delightful harmony of tastes and textures. From savory kebabs and flavorful shawarma to fresh salads and homemade hummus, each dish is crafted with care and precision, embodying the essence of Mediterranean cuisine. I enjoyed the Calamari as well as Panino's Chicken Apple Salad and it's a great place to grab a meal with family and friends.

CAVA

Cava might be a nationwide chain but it's also a refreshing take on fast-casual dining, offering a Mediterranean-inspired menu that allows you to create your culinary masterpiece. What stands out at Cava is the quality of ingredients. From the juicy, well-seasoned proteins to the array of vibrant vegetables and delectable sauces and hummus, each component bursts with freshness and flavor. Be sure to try the pita chips - they add the perfect crunch component to your meal.

ULELE

Ulele captures the essence of local flavors and history in a stunning waterfront setting along the Hilssborough River. The menu celebrates Florida's culinary heritage with a creative twist. Each dish showcases locally-sourced ingredients and pays homage to indigenous flavors. From their mouthwatering Native-inspired appetizers to the expertly prepared seafood and steaks, every plate is a fusion of innovation and tradition. You can try dishes here that you won't find elsewhere!

ON SWANN

For a refined take on American cuisine, On Swann is the place to go, exempling both elegance and culinary excellence. It's nestled in a chic and sophisticated setting that blends modern design with classic charm and they offer brunch, lunch, and dinner. Everything I've tried at this restaurant has been top tier so you definitely can't go wrong with whatever you pick!

TIKI DOCKS BAR & GRILL

Located along the Alafia River, this restauarant is a great place to spend a summer evening after work. The ambiance at Tiki Docks is vibrant and relaxed, with an expansive outdoor seating area overlooking the water. The tiki-themed decor creates a fun and laid-back atmosphere, making it a perfect setting for enjoying drinks, food, and good company. The menu offers a variety of classic bar and grill favorites, from tasty burgers and fresh seafood to flavorful appetizers, and my favorite is Coconut Shrimp.

MAPLE STREET BISCUIT CO.

This is one of my favorite brunch spots in Tampa, bringing Southern comfort food to a whole new level with its delectable biscuit-centric menu. Despite the name, they offer more than just biscuits including waffles, fritaffles, and more. The restaurant's ambiance exudes a cozy and inviting vibe, creating a welcoming space that feels like a gathering spot for friends and families, and from the moment you step inside, you're greeted by sweet smell of biscuits and syrup fills your. You can't really go wrong with any dish but my favorite is The Squawking Goat which was featured on Food Network!
